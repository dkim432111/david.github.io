The International Bureau of Statistics Readiness Scale

By Mark Stag | February 14 2068 12:02:00

The IBS has issued their most recent report of the Readiness Scale, which is a scale that depicts AI's suitability and coverage of specific industries and professions.

Credit: IBS 2068 RS Report


We can see the scale has changed since its first report nearly over a decade ago. There, service workers and software engineers were at the top. Since then, it seems that people have still maintained their service positions, as researchers predicted that entry-level positions would be preserved.

It seems that Creatives, which include painters, musicians, filmmakers, are at the most risk, with a significant increase in AI-Generated creative work a likely contributor to this shift.
